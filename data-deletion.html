Data Deletion Instructions

We do not store any personal data. However, if you want to remove any data associated with your Facebook interactions, follow these steps:

1. Remove App from Facebook

To delete your data, you must remove the app from your Facebook account:

  • Go to Facebook App Settings
  • Find the app and click "Remove"

2. Contact Us

If you have any concerns about data deletion, contact us at:

Email: support@i-gateway.net
